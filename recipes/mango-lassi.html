Mango Lassi

A tasty dessert drink from India.

This mango yogurt drink will be your new go-to for refreshment on a hot summer day!

Ingredients

  • 2 mangos - peeled, seeded and diced
  • 2 cups plain yogurt
  • Â½ cup white sugar
  • 1 cup ice

Directions

  1. In a blender, combine mangos, yogurt, sugar and ice.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour into glasses and serve.
